3D Printer 2 - Leaking

31-01-2014 by Gareth

3D-Printer
#hardware

Too Hot

While trying to calibrate my hot end I set my extruder speed too high which meant that the plastic was being pushed into the hot end faster than it could be extruded.

Back in business

After taking it apart I found that it was due to the inner PLA of the JHead slipping a bit inside. I have now reconstructed the hot end and its clean and tight.
